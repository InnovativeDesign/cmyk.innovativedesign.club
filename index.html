About

What is CMYK?

Join us on Saturday, October 27th, for a day of learning and practicing Graphic Design through a series of talks and friendly competition.

Just starting in your design journey? No sweat. We're excited to bring you a series of keynotes and workshops, presented by designers from Facebook, Salesforce, Course Hero, and more!

Work with team to create a Graphic Design campaign for a Bay Area company, and get feedback from designers before delivering an exciting 3-minute pitch for your designs!

Get ready for a day of learning, exploring and letting your creativity run wild — Come Make Your marK!

The event will be hosted at Jacobs Hall at the Jacobs Institute for Design Innovation. Coffee, bagels and lunch will be provided.
