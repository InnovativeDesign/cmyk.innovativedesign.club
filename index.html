Who is the client?

1951 Coffee Company, founded in 2015, is a nonprofit specialty coffee organization that promotes the well-being of the refugee community in the United States by providing job training and employment to refugees, asylees, and special immigrant visa holders while educating the surrounding community about refugee life and issues.

Their name derives from the year in which the UNHCR, at the 1951 Refugee Convention, first defined and set forth guidelines for the protection of refugees. These protections were further expanded in the 1967 Protocols giving the UNHCR a global mandate.

In the spirit of these conventions, 1951 Coffee Company was founded to address the unique challenges recently resettled refugees, asylees, and special immigrant visa holders face upon starting their new lives in the U.S. 1951 assists refugees in training for and attaining dignified, living-wage jobs that concurrently provide social, linguistic, and cultural orientation to their new U.S. community.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much design experience do I need?

There’s no expected level for your design experience! We encourage students from all levels to come out, whether they’re pursuing art as a hobby or an experienced graphic designer.

Do I need to sign up with a team in mind?

No need to sign up with a predetermined team! After the prompt release on the day of the event, we will group students who need teams together to collaborate.

What is the expected team size?

Each team needs to have a minimum of 2 members and can have up to a maximum of 5 members.

Where can I get design assistance?

Innovative Design holds office hours Monday - Friday at a table on Sproul from 10am - 2pm. Please swing by and ask us any questions. Speakers and Innovative Design volunteers will also be available on the day of the event to offer guidance. Additionally, we will have a table of our DeCal instructors ready to answer your Illustrator and Photoshop questions!

If I can no longer attend the event, can I receive a refund?

Unfortunately, all sales are final and we will not be able to issue any refunds. If you have any concerns, please reach out to innovativedesignatcal@gmail.com or message us on our Facebook page!
